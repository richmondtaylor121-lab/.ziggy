01 — Logo System

The Mark

The Bishop AI logo combines a geometric chess bishop icon with the wordmark in Poppins Bold. The icon represents strategic intelligence — the chess bishop moves diagonally, covering ground decisively. Handle with care.

Bishop AI
bishopai.io

Logo Rationale

The bishop chess piece is a direct expression of Bishop AI's identity: strategic, precise, and capable of decisive moves across the board. Unlike rooks or pawns, the bishop operates at angles — it sees things others don't. That's Bishop AI: oblique intelligence applied to revenue problems.

The amber-gold icon on deep navy creates immediate authority. The teal circuit detail signals AI/technology without being cliché. Poppins Bold for the wordmark is modern, geometric, and confident — never corporate or stiff.

04 — Iconography & Illustration

Visual Style

Icons and illustrations must feel cohesive with the overall brand — precise, modern, and purposeful. No clip art. No generic AI imagery.

Icon Weight & Style

Use regular weight line icons (1.5–2px stroke). Avoid ultra-thin or excessively bold variants. Rounded line caps and joins. Never mix icon styles within a single design — pick one library and stick to it. Recommended: Lucide, Phosphor (Regular), or Heroicons (Outline).

Illustration Style

Prefer abstract geometric graphics and data visualizations over literal illustrations. Node/graph diagrams, flow charts, and grid patterns work well with the brand aesthetic. Avoid: stock-photo AI imagery (glowing brains, humanoid robots), cartoonish illustrations, or overly ornate decorative elements.

Color in Icons

Icons default to Warm White (#EDE9E2) on dark backgrounds. Use Brand Gold (#E8B84B) to call attention to a single key icon per composition. Use Teal (#1BA8C8) for secondary or supporting icons. Never use more than two icon colors in one layout.

  • Are overly ornate or decorative with no functional purpose
  • Use colors outside the brand palette

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Speak

Bishop AI's voice is the same in every channel — only the tone shifts. Professional but human. Direct but not cold. Results-oriented but never arrogant.

Professional

Not corporate. Not stiff.

Write like a trusted expert who earns credibility through substance, not formality. Skip the jargon. Say what you mean.

Direct

Get to the point, always.

Lead with the outcome. Cut filler words. If a sentence doesn't add value, remove it. Every sentence earns its place.

Results-Oriented

Tie everything to outcomes.

Always connect features to impact. Don't describe what AI does — describe what it produces: more pipeline, less churn, faster ramp.

Collaborative

Human + AI together.

Emphasize partnership, not replacement. AI amplifies human capability — it doesn't remove the human from the equation.

08 — Brand Norms

The Rules

Firm rules specific enough to follow without interpretation. No exceptions without explicit approval from brand leadership.

Color Usage Norms

Gold (#E8B84B) is the primary brand color. Use for: H1–H2 headline accents, primary CTAs, logo icon, key data callouts. Limit to 1–2 focal points per layout.
Teal (#1BA8C8) is the secondary accent. Use for: links, secondary icons, chart lines, supporting data points. Never use for body text on dark navy — contrast ratio fails WCAG AA.
Deep Navy (#000D1A) is the canonical background in all primary brand contexts. Never replace with pure black (#000) or any other dark color.
Error Red (#EF4444) is reserved exclusively for errors, destructive actions, and alerts. It is never decorative.
Minimum contrast ratio for body text: 4.5:1 (WCAG AA). Warm White on Deep Navy: 15.8:1 ✓. Gold on Deep Navy: 8.1:1 ✓. Teal on Deep Navy: 3.9:1 — do not use for text.

Typography Norms

H1 is used for page titles only — one per page. Never use H1 for section headers or decorative text.
H2 is for primary section headers. One H2 per section. Poppins 700, ~32–40px.
Body text minimum: 16px (1rem), Open Sans 400, line-height 1.65–1.75. Never drop below 14px for sustained reading copy.
Maximum line length: 70–75 characters (approximately 600px at 16px). Longer lines harm readability and are a norms violation.
Never use more than two typeface families in a single composition (e.g., Poppins + Open Sans). Montserrat is permitted as a third only for navigation/label elements.
Letter spacing for label text (uppercase): minimum 0.14em. Body text: 0em (never tighten body copy).

Logo Norms

Clear space = the height of the capital 'B' in "Bishop" on all four sides. No element may intrude on this zone.
Minimum digital size: 120px wide (full lockup), 32px wide (icon-only). Minimum print size: 25mm wide (lockup), 8mm (icon-only).
The logo must never be recreated, redrawn, or approximated. Always use master vector files.
On light backgrounds, always use the Light Background variation with the dark icon. Never use the gold icon on white — it fails contrast standards.

Imagery Norms

Bishop AI does not use generic stock photography of people in offices, handshakes, or glowing laptop screens.
Preferred imagery: abstract data visualizations, node/network diagrams, geometric graphics, dashboard screenshots with real data, and genuine team photography (candid, not staged).
If photography is used, apply a dark duotone overlay using Deep Navy (#000D1A) and Brand Gold (#E8B84B) at 40% opacity to maintain brand consistency.
All images must be high resolution: minimum 2x (retina) for digital, minimum 300 DPI for print.
Never use AI-generated imagery of humanoid robots, cyborgs, or literal "brain = AI" metaphors. These are specifically prohibited.

09 — Brand Identity & Core Values

Who We Are

The principles that govern every decision — from color choices to the words we put in a cold email. These aren't aspirational. They're operational.

01

Activate, Don't Automate for Its Own Sake

Every AI system we build must activate measurable revenue outcomes. Technology that doesn't move a metric doesn't belong in the stack.

02

Amplify Human Capability

AI works beside people, not instead of them. We build systems that make humans faster, smarter, and more effective — never redundant.

03

Architect for Ownership

Every system we build is documented, transferable, and owned by the client. We don't create dependency. We create capability.

04

Align Strategy with Execution

GTM strategy without implementation is a slide deck. Implementation without strategy is wasted automation. We do both — and they must agree.

Brand Narrative
"Bishop AI helps businesses activate AI for revenue growth and better decision-making."

This is not a tagline. It is the operational mandate. Bishop AI works at the intersection of AI, revenue operations, go-to-market strategy, and automation. Every engagement produces systems — not recommendations. Every system produces results — not reports.


The brand exists to communicate capability without hype. In an industry full of promises about what AI will do someday, Bishop AI speaks only about what it has already done and what it will do on a defined timeline. Professional. Direct. Results-oriented. Collaborative.


Bishop AI is not a trend-chaser. It is a trusted advisor who delivers measurable outcomes — and the documentation to prove it.
