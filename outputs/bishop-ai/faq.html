Frequently Asked Questions

Read about our pricing philosophy, engagement processes, and what distinguishes Bishop AI from generic agencies.

We are founded by Richmond Taylor, a RevOps veteran who has configured tech stacks for high-growth sales teams. Unlike generic agencies that deliver simple Zapier connections, we focus on engineering robust database middleware (using n8n) and CRM structures (Salesforce/HubSpot) that scale without shatters.

Crucially, we do not lock you in. We train your internal staff to monitor, edit, and scale the workflows, giving you absolute autonomy.

We utilize a value-based framing rather than a standard hourly rate card. We assess the complexity of your current GTM database, the volume of manual hours we will reclaim, and the pipeline margin we will secure. You pay a fixed project fee for the build, and an audit is always completed first so you know exact returns before code is written.

Typically 4 to 8 weeks depending on database scale and API complexities. The first 2 weeks involve a thorough audit where we map GTM pipelines. We then build and test integrations inside staging environments before deploying to your live CRM, minimizing pipeline disruption.

We work with enterprise B2B sales and marketing technology. Our integration focus areas include:

  • CRM: Salesforce & HubSpot
  • Outreach nodes: Outreach.io, Instantly, and Aimfox
  • Middleware: n8n and custom API routes
  • Data & Intent: ZoomInfo, 6sense, Gong, and Drift
  • Communication: Slack, Chilipiper, and email engines

Our Tier II enablement consists of customized team training. We audit your SDR, AE, or RevOps teams' daily actions, build custom prompt templates and workflow files for your specific stack, and run interactive workshops. We reinforce this with 30 days of direct Slack support to resolve workflow blocks.

The fastest path is booking a strategy session directly via our Chilipiper scheduler calendar. We will review your current tools, identify high-leak pipelines, and outline an automation roadmap.
