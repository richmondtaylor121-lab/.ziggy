Tier I — Done-For-You

Automation Builds & GTM Integrations

Our done-for-you builds address the core operational leaks that growth-stage companies hit when manual processes scale faster than systems. We design and launch custom, event-driven workflows that eliminate copy-paste data tasks, manual CRM updates, and fragile logic loops.

SMBs with 5–100 employees and $500K–$10M in revenue. We have developed deep domain expertise within the following verticals:

Tier II — Capacity Building

Hands-On AI Training & Enablement

Software is only as good as the operators running it. Instead of simple prompting workshops, we build internal competencies within your operations team so they can build and maintain workflows themselves.

Target Teams & Outcomes

  • SDR & AE Teams: Practical prompt engineering for hyper-targeted account research, pre-call MEDDIC preps, and personalized email drafting.
  • RevOps & Ops Teams: Training on middleware architecture (n8n), database filtering, and API endpoint routing.
  • Leadership / Marketing: Workflows to monitor intent indicators, scale content operations safely, and parse analytics.

Training Engagement Process:

  1. Operational Gap Survey: We interview your team and audit their current daily tasks to locate inefficiencies.
  2. Custom Playbook Design: We draft a set of step-by-step prompt frameworks and workflow templates engineered for your exact tech stack.

  4. Ongoing Support Loops: Direct Slack support and bi-weekly office hours to troubleshoot new processes.
