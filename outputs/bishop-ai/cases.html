Case Study 01 Professional Services

Automated Handoffs for scaling B2B Agency

Client Context: A B2B consultancy with 45 employees scaling outreach databases across multiple sales divisions.

The Problem: When sales reps closed accounts, the onboarding details had to be copied by hand into Slack, Salesforce, and Google Sheets. Leads often sat unassigned for up to 4 days, resulting in client onboarding delays.

The Solution: We designed an n8n integration script. When an opportunity changes to "Closed Won" in Salesforce, it triggers database enrichment, creates Slack client rooms automatically, and assigns onboarding managers based on load.

The Results: Onboarding wait time decreased from 4 days to 6 minutes. Reps reclaimed 18 hours of manual logging every single week.

Tech Stack: Salesforce · n8n · Slack API · ZoomInfo

Case Study 02 E-commerce

Predictive CRM Remarketing Sync

Client Context: High-growth digital brand managing over 50,000 active customer records across multiple fulfillment databases.

The Problem: Sales systems did not connect with product stock changes. The marketing team was sending out manual emails advertising products that were temporarily out of stock, wasting budget.

The Solution: We linked their warehouse database with HubSpot and Salesforce CRM engines, running automated checks on inventory before launching outreach queues.

The Results: Prevented out-of-stock emails, reducing customer service support tickets by 42% and increasing repeat sales margins.

Tech Stack: HubSpot · custom APIs · Salesforce · Slack

Case Study 03 Real Estate

Dynamic Lead Routing Integration

Client Context: Commercial real estate firm managing lead pipelines from 5 distinct regional marketing channels.

The Problem: Lead assignment was manual, done once daily by the operations director. High-value leads sat in inbox queues for over 18 hours, resulting in low call-back compliance.

The Solution: We built a dynamic routing middleware layer using Chilipiper and Salesforce. Incoming leads are scored based on deal criteria and routed immediately to active agents.

The Results: Speed-to-lead dropped to under 3 minutes. Lead conversion rates increased by 28% in the first quarter.

Tech Stack: Chilipiper · Salesforce · Outreach · n8n

Case Study 04 B2B SaaS

Data Cleansing and Pipeline Alignment

Client Context: Venture-backed software firm scaling outbound SDR pipelines across 30 sales seats.

The Problem: Dirty outbound lists resulted in high bounce rates and domain warnings. SDRs were emailing inactive accounts, risking IP bans.

The Solution: We deployed an automated database cleaning engine that filters new leads through verification APIs and logs results in Salesforce before Outreach activation.

The Results: Email bounce rates fell to under 1.5%, securing domain health and increasing SDR call rates.

Tech Stack: Outreach · Salesforce · ZoomInfo · n8n
