Our Story

Founded on Engineering Precision

Bishop AI represents a shift in revenue operations—moving away from fragile temporary scripts toward hardened systems. Founded by Richmond Taylor, our mission is to build operations that run themselves.

The Founder

From Sales Technology to Autonomous Engines

Richmond Taylor has spent his career in the trenches of revenue operations, scaling technology stacks and databases for high-growth businesses. He has configured CRM rules, enrichment models, and dialing sequences for teams scaling from 10 to over 100 representatives.

The Moment of Shift: During a scaling cycle, Richmond observed a recurring barrier: companies were spending massive overhead to hire database admins and operations coordinators simply to copy and clean data. When API engines advanced, he realized that a single, custom n8n integration layer could automate what previously required a team of three operators.

He founded Bishop AI to bring this level of technical depth and systemic efficiency to B2B startups, professional services, and scaling vertical groups. Richmond remains heavily involved in design, ensuring that every build meets strict architectural requirements, but the agency is built as a complete operational partner designed to support clients long after setup.
