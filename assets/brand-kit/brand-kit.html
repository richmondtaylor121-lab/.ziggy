06 / Application Guidelines

Usage Instructions

Typography Rules

  • Header text is always rendered in Space Grotesk Bold, colored in Primary Green or Slate Grey.
  • Paragraphs use Plus Jakarta Sans, maintaining a minimum line height of 1.5.

Color Allocation Rules

  • Primary Green is the headline identifier. Never use it for full paragraphs of text.
  • Secondary Blue is reserved for text hyperlinks, outline borders, and clickable states.
  • Violet is a tertiary structural highlights tool (tags and monospaced indicators).

Imagery Style

  • Use clean, high-contrast user interface schematics, wireframes, and minimal 3D isometric vector art.
  • Avoid generic corporate stock photography. Use abstract representations of workflows instead.
07 / Voice Characteristics

Linguistic Rules Summary

Sentence Length:

Short, structured, and action-oriented. We favor direct commands and clear statements over long, complex paragraphs.

Formality Level:

Professional-Casual. We speak with high technical authority but maintain an open, approachable stance (e.g., using contractions like "we've" and "it's").

Humour Use:

Dry and clever. Humour is used occasionally as a sharp, witty undercurrent, avoiding cliché AI metaphors or cheesy puns.

Jargon Avoidance:

High. We explain advanced agentic concepts using plain developer terms or clear business utility, bypassing generic industry buzzwords.
